Mr Ayodele Adetoro

A man after God's own heart whom everyone loved

A photograph of Mr Ayodele Adetoro in his office

Here is a timeline of Mr Ayodele's life:

  • 1956 - Born in Osun state, Nigeria.
  • 1976 - After completing his seconday school education, he moved to Lagos.
  • 1972 - Gained admission into UNILAG to further his education where he studied banking and finance.
  • 1976 - He graduated from UNILAG and started his pursuit for a good job.
  • 1980 - He got a job in UBA as a banker where he worked till he met Mrs Olubunmi Adetoro.They courted for few years.
  • 1999 - He got married to Mrs OLubunmi Adetoro and God blessed their union with a boy and a girl.He loved his family so much and would give up anything for them.
  • 2006 - he died after a long fought battle of bone marrow anemia at age 50.
"Mr Ayodele Adetoro lived a live full of impact,he was hardworking, smart and very generouus.To know him was to love him because cared for everyone around him, everyone's worries became his as well and he always ensured he helped in any way he could.
-- Adetoro Adesewa
